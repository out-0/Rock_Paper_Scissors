# Rock Paper Scissors 🎮✊✋✌️

A simple **Rock Paper Scissors** game built with **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**.  
Play against the computer, test your luck, and see who wins!

---

## 🚀 How to Play
1. Open the game in your browser.
2. Click **Rock**, **Paper**, or **Scissors**.
3. The computer will also make a choice.
4. A popup will tell you if you **Win**, **Lose**, or **Draw**.

## 📜 Rules
- Rock beats Scissors  
- Scissors beats Paper  
- Paper beats Rock
